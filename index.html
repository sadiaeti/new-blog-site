How To Plan A Trip In 6 Simple Steps

How to Use Google Maps Trip Planner in 2024 (6 Easy Steps)

The mere thought of going on your coveted road trip fills you with thrills and excitement, doesn’t it? However, when planning this trip, it can be quite overwhelming, especially if you are solo traveling for the first time or a bunch of friends embarking on an unguided tour.

While you can always buy travel guides, get itinerary ideas online, or watch popular vlogs about your vacation destination, nothing can beat Google Maps trip planners in crafting your ideal travel plan. At least, I believe so!

Wondering why?

This is because using Google Maps not only helps you create a travel itinerary based on insights you gather from sources like vlogs or online searches, but it also helps you ‘actually’ execute it. And when I say it, I mean

Google Maps lets you:

  • Add your intended location
  • Color-code your location pins
  • Show multiple route options to reach them, along with estimated times of arrival
  • Save your favorite routes or places

In this blog, I will guide you through how I used Google Maps to plan my 3-day trip to Athens.

So, let’s get started.

Below is the step outline on how to use Google Maps to plan a trip:

Step 1: Open google maps
